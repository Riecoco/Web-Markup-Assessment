Become a Student Helper - Join the Karoton Mission!

Did you know? "Karoton" is the ancient Greek word for carrots. Just as carrots symbolize motivation in the phrase "the carrot and the stick," our mission is to inspire students to complete their IT courses—not with fear, but with confidence and excitement.

We believe learning should be enjoyable, not stressful. To achieve this, we need your help! If you're an experienced student who understands the challenges of freshman life, you can make a real difference by becoming a Student Helper.

What does a Student Helper do?

  • Connect with students via phone, email, or web conferencing.
  • Understand their needs and provide guidance or support.
  • Help us make IT learning more fun and approachable.

Your experience could be the key to unlocking someone else’s success! Interested? Get in touch today, and let’s work together to shape the future.
